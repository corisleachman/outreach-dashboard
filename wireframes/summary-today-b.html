Exploration B — design notes
No separate action block. The queue itself carries the hierarchy. The dominant card is just the first queue item, resized and reweighted.
The colour of the dominant card changes with context: cyan for replies, pink for drafts. The CTA button inside it is appropriate to the action.
Secondary items use the original .qi pattern — no visual change, just smaller relative to the top card. Nothing removed.
This approach keeps the page as a single unified surface — no conceptual separation between "the important thing" and "the rest".
Use the switcher to see how the top card changes when the most urgent item changes priority.
