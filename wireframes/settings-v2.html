Personality packs

Choose how your AI-generated messages are written. This sets your global default — you can override it per message or per sequence step.

This controls the writing style of messages sent to your prospects, not the product interface itself.

Calm & Commercial
Measured · Low pressure · Soft CTA
"Hi Sarah, I thought it might be useful to share something we've been seeing with teams in your space. Happy to explain if helpful."
Direct & Confident
Clear · Assertive · Strong CTA
"Hi Sarah, I'll cut straight to it — we help companies like yours build a repeatable pipeline in 90 days. Worth a conversation?"
Light Touch Relational
Warm · Conversational · No hard ask
"Hi Sarah, came across something that made me think of you — no agenda, just figured it might land at the right moment."
Challenger
Provocative · Bold · Reframes the problem
"Hi Sarah, most outreach fails before it starts. Here's what we're seeing work instead — takes 2 minutes to read."

Notifications

These appear briefly inside Pulse. as things happen — a small toast at the bottom of the screen, gone in a few seconds. No inbox, no badge, no email.

Replies from prospects land in your Gmail inbox as normal — Pulse. doesn't intercept them.
