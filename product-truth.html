Product truth

Pulse.

One page. Everything that matters. If the product drifts from this, it's wrong.

What Pulse is

Pulse helps you reach out to people consistently and turn that into replies.

It removes friction between having a list, sending messages, and following up.

What Pulse is not
  • A CRM
  • A pipeline manager
  • A data warehouse
  • A complex automation builder

If it starts behaving like one, it's drifting.

The core user loop
1
Add people
2
Write message
3
Sanity check
4
Send
5
Follow up
6
Respond

Everything in the product must support this loop.

The primary promise
"I can go from a list of people to messages being sent in minutes."
If anything slows that down, it's a problem.
The most important actions
Create campaign Import prospects Write message Approve Send

Everything else is secondary.

Design principles
Principle 1
Speed over ceremony
Users should move fast when they're confident.
Principle 2
Safety without friction
We provide confidence, not obstacles.
Principle 3
Defaults do the work
The system should make decisions for the user where possible.
Principle 4
One clear action per screen
Every surface answers: "What do I do here?"
Principle 5
Advanced features stay out of the way
Power exists, but never blocks the core flow.
Component truths
