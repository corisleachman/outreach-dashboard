Cold Email (Outreach. + Enrichment + Sequences)

Multi-touch email sequences to targeted, enriched lists.

🎯 Channel Goal

5–10 signups/month by Jun–Aug via:

  • Build targeted email lists (from multiple sources)
  • Enrich with valid email addresses (Hunter.io)
  • Multi-touch sequences (3–5 emails over 21 days)
  • Track opens/clicks/replies in outreach.

April: List Building & Enrichment Setup

Week 1–2: Define Target Segments

Identify 3–4 list sources:

  • List Source 1: Direct network + warm intros (100–200 people, high intent)
  • List Source 2: Company websites (50–100 agencies; scrape team pages)
  • List Source 3: Purchased/enriched B2B list (Apollo.io, ZoomInfo; 500–1000 emails)
  • List Source 4: Content-generated leads (blog, TikTok sign-ups; grows over time)

Week 3–4: Email Enrichment & Segmentation

Process:

  • Combine all lists into master spreadsheet (columns: First name, Last name, Email, Company, Title, Source, LinkedIn URL, Quality)
  • Enrich emails with Hunter.io (find missing addresses, verify validity)
  • Deduplicate and remove invalid emails
  • Segment by quality:
    • Segment A: High-intent (know you, warm intro, perfect ICP match) → 50–150 emails
    • Segment B: Medium-intent (similar role, adjacent industry) → 150–400 emails
    • Segment C: Broad (purchased list, broader targeting) → 200–500 emails

Note: Will send Segment A first (lowest risk, highest conversion); then B; then C.

May–August: Sequence Execution

Create 3 email sequence templates in outreach.:

  • Template 1 (Warm Intro): For Segment A (3 emails, day 0 → day 5 → day 12)
  • Template 2 (Soft Product Angle): For Segment B (3 emails, day 0 → day 7 → day 14)
  • Template 3 (Curiosity Hook): For Segment C (3 emails, day 0 → day 8 → day 16)

Full email copy is in the detailed playbook document. We'll detail each template in Q&A.

Send schedule:

  • Segment A: May week 1–2 (14/day, 2-hour windows)
  • Segment B: May week 3+ (30/day, 3-day send window)
  • Segment C: June onwards (50–75/day, 5-day send window)

Referral Program (Viral Loop)

Built into outreach., free to activate.

🎯 Channel Goal

3–5 signups/month by Jun; 8–12/month by Aug–Sep via:

  • In-app referral mechanism (1 month Pro free for both sides)
  • Email campaigns to early users
  • Social sharing buttons on beta page

May: Setup & Seed

Week 1–2: Create Referral Mechanism (Built into outreach.)

In-app setup:

  • Generate unique referral code per user (e.g., `CORIS_ABC123`)
  • Create shareable link: `outreach-app-theta.vercel.app?ref=CORIS_ABC123`
  • Message: "Invite a friend. You both get 1 month Pro free."
  • Track: `referred_by` column in users table (attribute signups to referrer)
  • Conversion: When referred user upgrades to Pro, award 1-month free to both

Week 3–4: Seed + Soft Campaign

Email to May cohort (existing users):

  • Subject: "Know someone who'd love outreach.? Get both 1 month free"
  • Send to users with 2+ campaigns sent (engaged users only)
  • Include unique referral link in email

In-app toast:

  • Trigger: After user sends first campaign
  • Message: "Great job! Know someone else doing cold outreach? Share this link—you both get 1 month Pro free."
  • One-time toast (doesn't repeat)

Jun–Aug: Monitor & Amplify

Weekly tracking (5 min/week):

  • Dashboard: How many referrals generated? How many converted to paid?
  • Calculate: Referral → signup conversion rate (target: 20–30%)
  • Recognize top referrers (optional, builds community)

Monthly email to all users:

  • Share "Referral leaderboard" (gamifies it)
  • Offer bonus: Top referrer gets extra Pro month + mention in newsletter
  • Builds FOMO; incentivizes sharing
