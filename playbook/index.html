🎯 LinkedIn Strategy Summary

Your ICP

  • Audience 1: Advertising agencies (London + NYC) — New Business Directors, Growth Directors
  • Audience 2: Recruitment/staffing firms (1–10 people) — Recruitment consultants, owners, BDs
  • Audience 3: Fractional operators/interim leaders — Managing multiple clients, need efficiency
  • Geography: UK + US from day 1
  • Dual purpose: Sales channel + network broadening vehicle

Your Multi-Channel Sequence

Day 1: Send 100–150 connection requests (spread across week) with optional warm, origin-story message
Day 4: Dripify sends DM #1 (question-based, build trust, ask about their pain)
Day 3–4: Send EMAIL (personally warm, short, origin story + soft CTA)
Day 8–9: Dripify sends DM #2 (references email, soft reminder)
Day 13–14: Dripify sends DM #3 (final touch, leave door open)
Anytime they respond: You take over manually (Dripify pauses automation)

Message Variants

  • Connection message: 3–4 variants (all warm, origin-story based, pain-focused) — rotate daily to avoid repetition + test
  • DM #1: 2–3 variants by audience (recruitment, agency, fractional) — question-based, build trust
  • Email: 2–3 variants by audience (same three) — personally warm, short, no jargon
  • DM #2 & #3: Same template for all (soft, understanding, no hard sell)

Cadence & Effort

  • Connection requests: 100–150/week, spread across 7 days (15–20/day)
  • Time commitment: 2–3 hrs/week (Dripify handles automation; you manage manual takeover on responses)
  • Variants: Rotate to test; measure and iterate weekly
  • Involvement: Dripify is 80% automated; you jump in on responses (your judgment, creativity, relationship-building)

📊 KPI Framework

Locked-In Targets

  • Connection acceptance rate: 5–8% (industry baseline is 3–5%; we're aiming above average)
  • DM response rate: TBD — measure first, then iterate based on data
  • Email click rate: TBD — measure first, then iterate
  • LinkedIn signups/week by June: 8–12/week (based on 100–150 connections × 5–8% acceptance × conversion funnel)

Weekly Tracking Checklist

  • Connection requests sent/week (target: 100–150)
  • Rolling acceptance rate (cumulative, target: 5–8%)
  • DM #1 response count + rate (measure by variant + audience)
  • Email open rate + click rate (measure by variant + audience)
  • Signups attributed to LinkedIn (via UTM or manual tracking)
  • Any emerging patterns? (Which variant is winning?)
  • Decision: Keep going, or change one variable? (Which?)

Decision Rules

  • If acceptance rate <3%: Retest CONNECTION MESSAGE angle
  • If DM response <5%: Try different DM variant or timing (Day 3 vs. Day 4)
  • If email click rate <2%: A/B test subject line or try shorter email
  • If zero signups after 2 weeks: Do 1:1 call with someone who engaged but didn't sign up; identify blocker
  • If acceptance rate >8%: Scale up to 200 requests/week; don't tinker with winning message
