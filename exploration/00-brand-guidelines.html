Foundation
Design Philosophy

Pulse. is built for consultants, agency owners, and fractional executives who win work through conversations. The design should feel like a system that celebrates momentum while keeping users grounded in practical execution. Calm, intentional, alive.

Core Principles
Rhythm & Momentum

Visual language mirrors the heartbeat of consistent outreach. Every interaction feels intentional, not decorative. The 8-second heartbeat cycle is the master rhythm — everything breathes at this rate or a harmonic of it.

70% Creative, 30% Minimalist

The audience are designers, agency founders, and business developers who value aesthetics but prize clarity. We lean creative but never sacrifice usability.

Celebration Without Gamification

The product celebrates real work, not engagement. Milestones matter. Streak pressure, gamification points, and forced engagement loops are explicitly out of scope — forever.

Explicitly excluded: Confetti animations · Streak pressure · Gamification points · Leaderboards · Blocking overlay celebrations · Sound effects · Forced engagement loops

Motion
Motion Principles

Motion carries emotional weight. Every animation is intentional, not decorative. The heartbeat is the master rhythm — everything breathes in sync with it or a harmonic.

Principle 1The product breathes. The 8-second heartbeat cycle is the master rhythm. Nothing should feel static or frozen.
Principle 2Invisible mechanics, visible life. The scan line is invisible. The effect is felt through cards responding to it — not through seeing the line.
Principle 3Ambient not distracting. All motion is background. It should register as "something feels alive" not "something is moving". When in doubt, reduce opacity and slow the timing.
Principle 4Functional motion. Amber pulse on follow-up cards. Cyan flash on new replies. Motion carries meaning — not just decoration.
Principle 5Consistent timing. New animations reference CYCLE_MS or its harmonics (4s, 2.5s). No ad-hoc durations.

Scan Effect

An invisible scan line travels bottom→top over exactly CYCLE_MS. When it passes within 60px of a scannable element's centre, that element's border briefly flares. The line itself is never visible — only the response is.

Button Interaction System

All primary CTA buttons share the same hover interaction. Two effects fire simultaneously on mouseenter — both are one-shot and don't repeat until the cursor leaves and re-enters.

Effect 1 — Ink Drop Bloom

A radial light bloom from the exact cursor entry point, expanding and fading like ink dispersing in water. Canvas 2D overlay appended to each button.

ParameterValue
DURATION1800ms
MAX_SCALE2.8× button diagonal
PEAK_AT0.08 (opacity peaks at 8% of duration)
Easingcubic ease-out: 1 - (1-t)³
Peak opacity0.22 — subtle internal bloom only
OriginExact cursor entry pixel — origin-aware
Effect 2 — Pulse Ring

A ring expands from the button border outward and dissolves. Lives on a wrapper div outside overflow:hidden so it scales beyond the button edge.

ParameterValue
Scale1.0 → 1.48
Peak opacity0.44
Duration1.2s
Easingcubic-bezier(0.2, 0.6, 0.3, 1)
Border1.5px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.55)
HTML structure<div class="btn-ring-wrap"> wrapping the button
Locked: No hover lift — translateY is explicitly removed from all button hover states. The button stays physically still. Effects respond, not the element. One-shot per hover.
Deferred: Pixel-displacement radial warp (distorting button shape/text as the ring travels) was explored. Not achievable at quality without WebGL/shader pipeline. Revisit when building the main app.

Decisions
Locked Decisions
LockedDesign direction: Electric Dreams. All other directions on hold.
LockedFonts: Outfit (headings/UI) + DM Sans (body) + DM Mono (code).
LockedDark mode default. Light mode available as user preference (Phase 2.19+).
LockedButton hover: Ink drop bloom + pulse ring. No translateY lift.
LockedScan mechanic: Linear sweep (electric6). Velocity-linked scan rejected and closed.
LockedLogo: "Pulse" white + "." in gradient. Outfit 800. Dot is part of brand identity.
LockedTagline: "The heartbeat of outreach."
LockedNo celebration bloat: Confetti, streaks, gamification points — permanently excluded.
LockedLight mode thread: #faf8ff background shares the purple family with #0d0914. Same product in different light.
LockedParticle trail (electric4): Explored. Too busy alongside scan. Dropped from canonical. Closed.
Decisions
Deferred — Not Dismissed
DeferredButton warp effect: Pixel-displacement warp as pulse ring travels. Needs WebGL/shader pipeline. Revisit in main app.
DeferredQuiet Luxury direction: Domaine Display + Circular Std. Paid fonts. On hold.
DeferredNeo-Naturalism direction: Saol Text + Maax. Paid fonts. On hold.
DeferredSpace Grotesk: Explored for headings. Strong 70s editorial energy. Not dismissed — worth revisiting if direction shifts.
DeferredForm field scan (B2): Input fields in the access section getting individual border flares as scan passes.
Planned Feature
Brand Personalisation

Users will be able to personalise the visual appearance of their Pulse. dashboard — gradient colours and background — so the tool feels like theirs. Free tier feature, deliberately. Personalised workspaces drive stickiness.

Prototype live: brand-explorer.html — full marketing site with live colour pickers, gradient generator, and background colour switcher. This is the design reference for the Settings UI.
